## Nick Dewaele


### About me

<table><tr><td width="400"><img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Nikdwal/nikdwal.github.io/master/writing.jpg"></td><td> I obtained my doctorate at KU Leuven on 05/07/2024.
